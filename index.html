Talo Homes FAQs

Where is Talo located?

Talo is located just off Highland Drive and 3300 South. The exact address of the project is 3780 S Highland Drive, Millcreek, Utah. Millcreek is a progressive, newly established township. Near Talo is an area called Brickyard. Millcreek city government and local developers have released a master plan to redevelop Brickyard as a mix-used project. This new zoning will bring new shops, restaurants, and consumers to the heart of Millcreek. Your home at Talo will be just .8 mile from all this new development.

Who is the Developer?

https://www.blueatlashomes.com
Blue Atlas Homes is developing Talo. Brendon MacKay established Blue Atlas Homes as a Design+Build firm with a focus on fine craftsmanship, clean style, and innovative design details. Before entering the residential market, Brandon worked in the auto industry, designing award-winning cars as well as products for the commercial market. This experience has served as an inspiration which allows for unique designs in complex and demanding environments. The Talo development showcases the “out of the box” concepts for which Blue Atlas Homes has become known.

Who is the architect and interior designer?

Russell Platt Architecture is a full-service architectural and design firm in Salt Lake City. The firm brings a over 50 years of experience in both large and small-scale projects throughout the region. Their leadership includes award-winning licensed architects, seasoned architectural and interior designers, CAD drafters, planners, graphic designers, cost estimators, and several intern architects. Many of Russell Platt’s professionals are LEED® Accredited with projects designed with a mindset toward sustainability. Russ Platt, Principal Architect, has thoughtfully created Talo’s modern design, social spaces, and interiors.
